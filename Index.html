Our History

EcoBrew Coffee Co. was founded in 2015 in the beautiful town of Wolfeboro, New Hampshire by Makenzie Kavanagh. Inspired by summers spent on the shores of Lake Winnipesaukee, Makenzie saw firsthand the importance of protecting our natural bodies of water and surrounding environment. What began as a single coffee stand with a mission to reduce waste has grown into a company dedicated to sustainability. From compostable cups to shoreline cleanups, EcoBrew is built on the belief that great coffee can support a cleaner planet, both on land and in our lakes.

Why Use Us?

  • We are locally founded in Wolfeboro, NH, and deeply committed to protecting the beauty of Lake Winnipesaukee and our surrounding environment.
  • All of our products are sustainably sourced and packaged using compostable or recyclable materials.
  • We organize regular shoreline and community cleanups to give back to the land and water that inspire us.
  • We partner with local farms and vendors to reduce our carbon footprint and support the regional economy.
  • We align our operations with the UN Sustainable Development Goals, particularly Clean Water, Responsible Consumption, and Climate Action.
